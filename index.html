Arrow keys to move, Up to launch
Created by Myles (8), Samantha (11), and Jason O'Leary (I don't wanna talk about)

How to Play

Controls

Left / Right Arrow Keys: Move the paddle

Up Arrow Key: Launch the ball

R: Restart after game over

ESC or H: Open/close this help screen

Objective

Destroy all bricks to win! You start with 3 lives. Lose a life when the ball falls below the paddle. Game over when all lives are lost.

Scoring

4 HP +1 +1 +1 +2

Top row: 4 HP bricks (5 pts total)

2 HP +1 +2

Middle rows: 2-3 HP bricks (3-4 pts total)

1 HP +1

Bottom row: 1 HP bricks (1 pt each)

Points: +1 for each hit, +2 bonus for destroying multi-hit bricks

Brick Damage

No damage
1 hit
2 hits
3 hits

Cracks appear as bricks take damage

Paddle Acceleration

300 600 900

Speed (px/s) — Holding a direction accelerates from 300 to 900

Changing direction resets to base speed (300). Paddle movement influences ball bounce angle.

Ball Collision Physics

Center hit: straight up

Edge hit: angled bounce

Paddle bounce: Angle depends on where the ball hits. Center = straight up, edges = angled. Moving paddle adds extra horizontal velocity (30% influence).

Wall bounce: Perfect reflection with anti-stuck prevention.

Brick bounce: Reflects off the side the ball entered from.

Tips

  • Use paddle speed to control ball angle
  • Bricks crack visually as they take damage
  • Top row bricks need 4 hits to destroy
  • Bottom row bricks are easiest targets
